Sci-fi Books Recommendation

A brief list of my favorite sci-fi books:

  1. The Songs of Distant Earth by Arthur C. Clarke
  2. The Ultimate Hitchhiker's Guide by Douglas Adams
  3. Ender's Game by Orson Scott Card
  4. Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Sea by Jules Verne
  5. The Three-Body Problem by Cixin Liu
  6. Story of Your Life by Ted Chiang
  7. The Martian Chronicles by Ray Bradbury

All these science fictions are famous award-winning novels. Personally, I prefer The Three-Body Problem the most because it introduces a lot of physics and astronomy knowledge, including proven laws and hypotheses. Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Sea is also amazing as I enjoy reading adventure novels too. Anyway, all these fiction books are undoubtedly well worth reading.
